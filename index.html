LOVE BREAKFAST CAKES:

Love all things breakfast but don't have the time to sit through a three or four-course meal in the morning? Our Love Breakfast Cakes are the salty-and-sweet satisfying balance to get you (and your taste buds) going—and stay on-the-go in the mornings.

Forget low-quality made-to-order breakfast sandwiches that are just microwaved and wrapped, then handed in a paper bag through a drive-thru window. Those below-average breakfasts are not the on-the-go bite you need to power up your morning. Our Baked™ Love Breakfast Cakes are perfect for your commute or your quick sit-down meal to fuel up for your workday.

Can't choose between savory or sweet? You don't have to—layered with a timely scrambled egg, American cheese, your choice of meat, and our homemade blueberry jam, the pancake sandwich is then heated and wrapped to perfection.

CHICKEN AND WAFFLES:

In this Southern breakfast classic, our Chicken and Waffles is a sweet and savory breakfast blend that has and will always stand the test of time.

Our Baked™ (but actually) fried chicken is mixed with our favorite personal seasoning, soaking up all the best homemade flavors. Covered in a crispy crunch, it's then fried to perfection and encased in a hot and toasty coating for the perfect side to our homemade waffles.

Let's talk about those waffles—because at Baked Café, we take breakfast (or anytime dining) seriously, and this doesn't change with the art of our homespun waffle batter. Leaving you with a not-too-sweet bite of doughy goodness, our waffles can be eaten on their own or drizzled with delectable sticky syrup to bring even more sweetness to your plate.

Put together, our Baked™ Chicken and Waffles bring all aspects of taste to your palate, from sweet to salty, from crunchy to soft to doughy, you have everything you need on this plate of deliciousness.
